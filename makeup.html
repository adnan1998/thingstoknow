5 epic tips to look fresh.


Posted by CLARRISA BUTERA on FEBRUARY 5, 2018

I knw i know you guys are like “oh you know we bath every day” and stuff after reading the title and trust me this isnt what i mean. Its just something you can add to your makeup list or daily routine to look fresh till the end of the day!


1. Use kajal/dark or black eyeshadow under your eyes in a very small amount.
2. Use light lipsticks like light pink or light orange.
3. Avoid foundation or pink coloured cheeks
4. Use tops which are slightly hanging from your ears
5. Use light lenses like – blue or light green

Yes it does sound a bit stupid i wont blame you but in your free day just apply these tips i swear just give it a chance you will see the difference.
